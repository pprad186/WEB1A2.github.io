About Us

We aspire to make you comfortable and confident in your steps.

Our Story

BOOTS began with a simple belief: that every girl deserves boots that not only look exceptional but feel extraordinary. Founded in 2025 by Prerana, our company emerged from a shared frustration with the compromise between quality and affordability in the boots market, as well as a strong desire to look fabulous.
Learn about our journey!


Our Values


Quality First

We source only the finest materials. Every stitch, every zipper, every detail is carefully considered and executed in perfection.

Sustainability

We're committed to responsible manufacturing practices, using eco-friendly materials, and ensuring our production processes minimize environmental impact.

Innovation

We continuously explore new manufacturing methods, we continuously explore new techniques to improve comfort, durability, and style.

About our products

Our Boots are made from 100% pure leather and 100% merino wool inner lining for exceptional warmth and softness. The classic patterns add timeless texture and visual interest to our versatile pieces. Natural temperature regulation properties make it perfect for transitional seasons.
